ABOUT Partner-cons

Partner-cons offers business software solutions for every company looking for more value and more features from their ERP solutions. Since 2011 we have been delivering web-based ERP software solutions to small and mid-sized companies as well as international enterprises. Partner-cons is an end-to-end solution that supports most industries with its complete ERP software. Partner-cons solution is flexible enough to run on any operating system and SQL database. Partner-cons solution delivers outstanding value based on exceptional price/performance and functionality. With Partner-cons modular design, customers can select the software capabilities that best meet their current business needs, with the opportunity to expand in the future within the ERP software suite.

Mission

Partner aims at improving the profitability and efficiency of local businesses through specialised business software that guides decision makers, and enhances users’ productivity. Partner plays an active role in Software Technology, and aspires to elevate our region from technology consumption, to a more active role that contributes to global technology making.

vision_about

The vision_about of the company is to provide complete integrated solution to the organisation and to become the most preferred technology partner in the industry. We will continuously strive to supply solutions to meet expectations of our customers in achieving their business objectives by sustained emphasis on best cost, technology, timely and quality services
Partner recognises the crucial role of customer service in the IT field, and considers after-sales service an integral part of its products. Through a network of branches and business partners, Partner is able to serve customers in all GCC states and Egypt, and plans to extend its services to cover all Arab countries. In order to achieve these ambitious targets, Partner Adapts a strategy that includes producing high quality applications, to stand head -to-head with international products in quality and attention to fine details, while keeping focused on the needs and requirements of local users and businesses. Today, many businesses rely on Partner-cons to run their operations effortlessly, while Partner re-aligns its efforts and investment to become the largest business software house in the Middle East, and to extend its reach to international frontiers. To achieve this aim, Partner stands firmly on the following simple foundation:
  1. Continuous development to our solutions using the latest software engineering technologies.
  2. Focus and emphasis on the business requirements of our region.
  3. Improve customer service to help users carry out better business with less effort.
  4. Extend our coverage by opening up service centres throughout the region

Partner Software Development Strategy

Partner aims at improving the way business is run in the Arab area through high quality ERP and accounting software solutions that are designed and developed specifically for the Middle East. To achieve this target, Partner has adapted a clear strategy that guides its development process. Partner Strategy for software development broadly based on four principles that clearly distinguish Partner from its competitors.

Specialization

ERP solutions or business software in general is fundamentally different from desktop applications and utilities. The development, maintenance, and support environment of applications that deal with the lifeline of businesses differ greatly in structure and requirements from applications like word processors and internet browsers. Partner recognises this fact, and has specialised in developing and supporting business software only since its start. This attitude has enabled Partner to acquire and accumulate a tremendous amount of experience and know-how that relate to developing, supporting and maintaining business software. This experience is automatically transferring to our CLIENTs as we serve them, and at the same time “steers” Partner internal processes towards satisfying local business needs and requirements.
